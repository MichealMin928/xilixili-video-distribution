# Content production system

Read this reference before creating, revising, or evaluating a Xilixili hotel-projector article, gallery, platform copy set, or content queue.

## Define the content unit

Assign a stable ID in the form `XLL-YYYY-NNN`. Make each item answer:

1. One customer decision node.
2. One specific buyer problem.
3. One traceable evidence source or reproducible check.
4. The allowed claim and prohibited extrapolation.
5. One useful next action or downloadable checklist.
6. The minimum project information needed for follow-up.

Use the pattern **one problem + one piece of evidence + one action**. Put topics without sufficient evidence in a pending-evidence pool instead of writing generic promotional claims.

## Build the seven-page master

- Use 1080×1440 pages with a readable mobile safe area.
- Make the cover filter for the intended hotel buyer and state the problem.
- Keep one core judgment per inside page.
- Use diagrams or real authorized evidence; do not invent a technical scene that could misstate installation direction.
- Put scope limits near the claim they qualify.
- End with a concrete checklist, measurement form, or project-intake packet rather than a generic “contact us.”
- Export all PNG pages and a seven-page overview, then inspect the overview and critical pages at full size.

## Create four native platform cards

Share facts, evidence, media, and claim boundaries. Rewrite the hook, order, title, action, tags, music, and acceptance criteria:

- Douyin: diagnostic problem language and search intent; five tags.
- Xiaohongshu: collectible checklist or calculation; five tags.
- Kuaishou: engineer and field language; four tags and the approved instrumental rule.
- WeChat Channels: complete project context and handoff; three tags.

Do not treat base-copy truncation as a native rewrite. Keep the base manifest portable, and store platform editorial intent in `平台文案.md`.

## Gate facts and approval

- Track brand cooperation, product parameters, cases, and numbers with source, scope, allowed wording, prohibited extrapolation, review date, and related content IDs.
- Freeze content when a relied-on fact expires or conflicts with newer evidence.
- Default every new manifest to `approvedForAutoPublish: false` outside `content/approved/`.
- Creation and review do not authorize prefill, scheduling, or publishing.

## Record results without reading interactions

Link the content ID to each platform work ID or URL and its 1h, 24h, and 7d public metrics. The account owner may manually enter counts for qualified inquiries, appointments, quotes, and deals. Never read or store comment or private-message text for routine attribution.

Prefer qualified hotel inquiries and business progression over views. Do not scale a high-view topic when it attracts the wrong audience. Observe small samples rather than changing strategy from one post.

## Delay matrix expansion

Do not add account slots merely to repeat a thin content queue. Expand only after the team maintains an approved content buffer, native platform variants, content-to-business attribution, explicit owners and approvers, and isolated browser/state boundaries for each account group.
